What We're Made Of

Our Eco-Friendly Materials

♻️
Recycled PET Fabric

Made from post-consumer plastic bottles — each metre of fabric diverts ~8 bottles from landfill. Soft, durable, and fully traceable.

Used in: Glass Slipper, Timeless Companion
🌿
Organic Cotton

GOTS-certified cotton grown without synthetic pesticides. Uses 90% less water than conventional cotton and supports healthy soil.

Used in: Storykeeper, Timeless Companion
🪡
Reclaimed Silk

Sourced from deadstock fashion houses and surplus mills. Zero new silk production — vintage luxury, zero waste.

Used in: Golden Thread, Timeless Companion
🥿
Upcycled Leather Scraps

Offcuts from European leather goods manufacturers that would otherwise be incinerated. Each piece is unique by nature.

Used in: Glass Slipper Muse
⚙️
Recycled Metals

925 recycled sterling silver and brass. Conflict-free, traceable, and certified by the Responsible Jewellery Council.

Used in: Infinite Gem Accessories
🌾
TENCEL™ Lyocell

Wood-pulp fibre from sustainably managed forests. Closed-loop production — 99% of water and solvents are recycled.

Used in: Dragonheart Ethic Line
