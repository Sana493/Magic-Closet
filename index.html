Once upon a time…

The Spell Behind
the Magic

I always used to think fashion was a kind of magic. Like opening a spellbook and choosing who you wanted to become that day. Every outfit felt like a transformation.

But as I grew older, I saw a very different side. Fast fashion — clothes designed, produced, and discarded at overwhelming speed. The people who make them, the impact left behind — all invisible.

What if fashion could still feel like opening a spellbook — but instead of waste and speed, it was about purpose, ethics, and transparency? What if there were modern-day fairy godmothers — not with wands, but with strategy and storytelling?

That idea became Spell Book Agency, and this is our gift to the world: Magic Closet.

🤝 Fair Labour

  • All workers paid living wages — min 30% above local market rate
  • Zero tolerance for child labour across the entire chain
  • Maximum 8-hour days with paid holidays
  • Workers have the right to organise and form unions
  • Safe and hygienic conditions verified quarterly

🌍 Community Impact

  • 60+ artisan communities supported across 12 countries
  • 3% of revenue reinvested into community education
  • Women's Cooperative Networks in India and Ghana
  • Artisan stories shared with every product purchase
  • Annual community impact report published publicly

🔍 Radical Transparency

  • Full traceability from raw fibre to finished product
  • Factory audit reports published publicly
  • Every product QR code links to its production journey
  • Prices broken down: material, labour, shipping, margin
  • Emissions data verified by third parties annually

💜 Animal Welfare

  • 100% cruelty-free — no fur, no exotic skins, no angora
  • RWS-certified wool from farms with welfare audits
  • Peace silk only, never conventional silk
  • Leather alternatives prioritised; recycled leather only
  • Active supporter of the Fur Free Alliance
