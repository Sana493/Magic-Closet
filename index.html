The Spell Book Agency

Fashion That Feels Like Magic

We always believed fashion was a kind of magic — like opening a spellbook and choosing who you wanted to become. But we saw how fast fashion stripped that magic away. So we asked: what if fashion could still feel enchanting, but be purposeful, ethical and transparent?

That question became Spell Book — a creative digital agency acting as modern fairy godmothers for fashion brands. And Magic Closet is our vision brought to life.

  • 🗺️
    Full Supply Chain Transparency — Every item includes sourcing details, artisan wages, and carbon footprint data.
  • ♻️
    Circular Economy Program — Return worn items for store credit and recycling rewards.
  • 🤝
    60+ Artisan Partners — Ethical manufacturers who pay living wages in safe conditions.
  • 🌿
    1% for the Planet — Every purchase contributes to environmental regeneration worldwide.
